About Me
Data Analyst with a
Business-First Mindset

I didn't start in data analytics. I started in real estate sales — and that's exactly what makes me different.

For 2.5 years I was on the ground every day, managing 50+ customers weekly, closing 4–5 deals a month, and constantly reading market patterns — what customers wanted, where the market was moving, what competitors were doing. I was doing analysis without even calling it that.

Now I'm finishing my Master's in Computer Science at William Jessup University in California, building my technical toolkit: Power BI, Excel, SQL, Python, R, and Tableau. I enjoy it because I can directly connect every tool to real business problems I've already lived through.

What I bring that most entry-level analysts don't — I know how businesses actually operate from the inside. I know how to talk to non-technical teams. And I know how to work hard, not just behind a screen.
